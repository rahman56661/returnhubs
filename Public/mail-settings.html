🔐 SMTP Email Credentials

These credentials will be used to send automated emails from your email account.

💡 Regular password won't work. You need to generate an "App Password" for security.

📋 How to Get App Password

Gmail
Outlook
Yahoo
  1. Go to your Google Account
  2. Click on Security in the left navigation panel
  3. Under "Signing in to Google", select 2-Step Verification
  4. Scroll down and click on App passwords
  5. Select Mail as the app and Other (Custom name) as the device
  6. Enter "Warehouse System" as the name and click Generate
  7. Copy the 16-character password and paste it in the password field above
  1. Go to your Microsoft Account Security
  2. Click on Advanced security options
  3. Under "App passwords", click on Create a new app password
  4. Enter "Warehouse System" as the app name
  5. Click Create and copy the generated password
  6. Paste the password in the field above
  1. Go to your Yahoo Account Security
  2. Scroll down to "App passwords" section
  3. Click on Generate app password or Manage app passwords
  4. Select "Other" as the app and enter "Warehouse System" as the custom name
  5. Click Generate and copy the password
  6. Paste the password in the field above
